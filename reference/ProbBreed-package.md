# The 'ProbBreed' package.

ProbBreed uses probability theory under the Bayesian framework for
calculating the risk of selecting candidates in a multi-environment
context. Contained are functions used to fit a Bayesian
multi-environment model (based on the available presets), extract
posterior values and maximum posterior values, compute the variance
components, check the model’s convergence, and calculate the
probabilities. For both across and within-environments scopes, the
package computes the probability of superior performance and the
pairwise probability of superior performance. Furthermore, the
probability of superior stability and the pairwise probability of
superior stability across environments is estimated.
